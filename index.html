FAQ

Questions, answered

How quickly will I receive my deliverables?

Standard SLA is 72 hours from brief confirmation. Pro and Agency retainers get 48h and same-day priority respectively. Full Suite runs are prioritised.

What if the leads don't match my ICP?

We offer a free revision if more than 20% of the leads fall outside the agreed ICP criteria. Your alignment brief is the foundation — we honour it.

Can I start on-demand and move to a retainer?

Yes. Most clients begin with an on-demand Argus + Lynx run, then upgrade to a retainer once the signal-driven workflow is embedded.

What does "first run free for qualified agencies" mean?

Agencies running outbound for clients can receive a free Argus Starter run. We confirm eligibility within 24 hours of your brief.

In what format are deliverables sent?

Argus runs arrive as PDF + DOCX. Lynx workspaces are shared via link. Intelligence briefs include a document plus a live analyst session.

Do you offer custom builds?

Yes. For teams with specific data-source, CRM integration or workflow requirements, we design custom builds on request.
