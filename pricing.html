FAQ

Common
questions.

How quickly will I receive my deliverables?

Standard SLA is 72 hours from ICP brief confirmation. Pro and Agency retainers are 48h. Full Suite runs are prioritised.

What if the leads don't match my ICP?

We offer a free revision on any run where more than 20% of leads fall outside the agreed ICP criteria. Your brief is binding — so are we.

Can I start on-demand and move to a retainer?

Yes. Most clients start with an Argus Starter or Argus + Lynx run to validate the engine, then move to a retainer after the first qualified conversation.

What does "first run free for qualified agencies" mean?

Agencies running outbound for clients qualify for a complimentary Argus Starter run. Send us a brief and we'll confirm eligibility within 24 hours.

In what format are deliverables sent?

Every run includes a PDF and a DOCX file. Lynx workspaces are accessed via a shared link. Intelligence briefs include a written document plus the live session.

Do you offer custom builds?

Yes — for teams with specific data sources, CRM integrations, or workflow requirements. Contact us to scope a custom build.
